AT THE FOOT OF THE HELDERBERG MOUNTAIN, SITUATED BETWEEN HERITAGE WINE FARMS AND NATURE RESERVE, YOU CAN FIND THE PERFECT WAY OF LIVING: ERINVALE.


Since launch in 1995 Erinvale Country and Golf Estate established its ranking amongst the TOP Estates in South Africa. A true gem in the Western Cape with 419 properties — ranging from smaller family homes to spacious villas — all carefully positioned alongside beautifully maintained green lawns and blue dams of the prestigious Erinvale Golf Course — designed by Gary Player.

Erinvale combines the best of all: The benefit of excellent security in a gated community, the tranquility of the surrounding nature, a world class golf course on your doorstep and the various amenities of the Estate and other local facilities that translate into a 5-Star Lifestyle.

Gary Player:

ERINVALE COUNTRY & GOLF ESTATE

Erinvale is a low density Estate and the 419 homes are generously built on 284 hectares of land with breathtaking views towards the Hottentot Mountains, the Winelands, the Helderberg Mountain and across False Bay. Whilst offering this pristine natural environment, Erinvale is connected with the latest generation FTTH high speed internet and is only 30-40 minutes away from the CPT International Airport and Cape Town City Center, as well as being in close proximity to Municipal infrastructure, excellent schools, and local shopping facilities in Somerset West and Stellenbosch.

Erinvale Country and Golf Estate provides a privileged lifestyle for a large variety of residents: Golfers, younger families, mid-aged professionals and retired people — all enjoying the freedom of a peaceful, safe, and valuable environment.

ERINVALE GOLF CLUB

One of the jewels of Cape Winelands golf, Erinvale is a 'must play' golf course nestled at the foot of the Helderberg mountains, with sweeping views of the Winelands and False Bay coastline. The Gary Player designed course was opened in 1995 by "the Master himself", and since established its position amongst the top golfing experiences in the Western Cape. The course has an interesting and varied layout, and the back nine must be considered amongst the best in the country.

The club provides excellent warm-up facilities including a driving range, chipping and putting greens, as well as a fully-equipped pro-shop offering local and international brands. The setting, the course, the facilities, and the friendly people all make for a unique golfing experience that will bring you back to Erinvale.

ERINVALE EXPERIENCES

There are many exciting experiences in and around Erinvale Estate to be explored — just in walking distance.

The Helderberg Nature Reserve offers 403 hectar of pristine Fynbos landscape, a large variety of Protea Plants and over 170 bird species and other mammals. Multiple walking and hiking trails with different difficulties invite with fantastic views and bird watching opportunities.

Erinvale Residents have direct access from the Estate into the Nature Reserve to enjoy anything from a relaxed walk to an adventurous hiking day around Westpeak and the Dome – the Top of the Helderberg.
The acclaimed wine farms Vergelegen, Morgenster, and Lourensford are direct neighbours and offer exquisite restaurants, wine and olive oil tastings, guided Tours, various Trails, open air concerts and music festivals, weekend markets and so much more.
