AT THE SLOPES OF THE HELDERBERG MOUNTAIN, SITUATED BETWEEN HERITAGE WINE FARMS AND NATURE RESERVE, YOU CAN FIND THE PERFECT WAY OF LIVING: ERINVALE.


Since launch in 1992 Erinvale Country and Golf Estate established its ranking amongst the TOP Estates in South Africa. A true gem in the Western Cape with 419 properties — ranging from smaller family homes to spacious villas — all carefully positioned alongside beautifully maintained green lawns and blue dams of the prestigious Erinvale Course — designed by Gary Player.

Erinvale combines the best of all: The benefit of great security in a gated community, the tranquility of the surrounding nature, a world class golf course on your doorstep and the various amenities of the Estate and other local facilities that translate into a 5 Star Lifestyle.

Gary Player:
"...when I first layed eyes on Erinvale, I was struck by the incredible beauty of the farm and the potential for a golf course of world-class standards!"

ERINVALE COUNTRY & GOLF ESTATE

Erinvale Estates' low density residential developement comprises 419 homes generously built on 284 hectares (2.84km²) of land with breathtaking views towards the Hottentot Mountains, the Winelands, the Helderberg Mountain, and across False Bay. Whilst offering this pristine natural environment, Erinvale is connected with the latest generation FTTH high speed internet and is only 30-40 minutes away from the CPT International Airport and Cape Town City Center, as well as being in close proximity to Municipal infrastructure, excellent schools, and local shopping facilities in Somerset West and Stellenbosch.

Erinvale Country and Golf Estate provides a privileged lifestyle to residents of all age levles: ...younger families, mid-aged professionals, and retired people — all enjoying the freedom of a peaceful, safe, and valuable environment.

ERINVALE GOLF CLUB

One of the jewels of Cape Winelands golf, Erinvale is a 'must play' golf course nestled at the foot of the Helderberg mountains, with sweeping views of the Winelands and False Bay coastline. The Gary Player designed course was opened in 1995 by "the Master himself", and since established its position amongst the top golfing experiences in the Western Cape. The course has an interesting and varied layout, and the back nine must be considered amongst the best in the country.

The club also offers brilliant warm-up facilities including a driving range, chipping and putting greens, as well as a good pro shop with a selection of clothing, shoes, clubs, and accessories. The setting, the course, the facilities, and the friendly people all make for an admirable golf experience that will bring you back to Erinvale.

ERINVALE EXPERIENCES

There are many exciting experiences in and around Erinvale Estate to be explored — just in walking distance.

The Helderberg Nature Reserve — which Erinvale Residents can directly access to explore the various trails of the Nature Reserve, offering anything from a relaxed walk trhrough the Protea and Fynbos vegetation to an adventurous hiking day around the dome: The top of the Helderberg.

The acclaimed wine farms Vergelegen, Morgenster, and Lourensford are direct neighbours and offer exquisite restaurants, wine and olive oil tastings, guided Tours, various Trails, open air concerts and music festivals, weekend markets and so much more.
